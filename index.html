Vision

The mission of the Black Student Union at Interlake High School is to foster a supportive and empowering space that nurtures community, representation, and opportunity for Black students. We are committed to creating an environment where members feel safe, respected, and encouraged to share their experiences, voices, and perspectives.

BSU works to strengthen community by hosting discussions, cultural events, and social activities that promote connection and mutual support. Through intentional programming, we celebrate Black history, culture, and achievements while encouraging critical conversations about identity, equity, and social justice.

A core part of our mission is to provide Black students with access to mentorship and role models who reflect their aspirations. By organizing guest speakers, workshops, and partnerships, we expose students to diverse professional, academic, and creative pathways. These experiences help prepare members to navigate both professional and casual settings with confidence, cultural awareness, and self-advocacy.

Additionally, BSU is dedicated to leadership development and personal growth. We aim to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to succeed beyond high school while maintaining pride in who they are. Through community, education, and empowerment, Interlake BSU strives to ensure Black students are prepared to thrive wherever their futures lead.
